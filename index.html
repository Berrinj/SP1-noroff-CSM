Explore together

Welcome to Trondheim Community Science Museum, a place where curiosity knows no bounds and learning becomes an interactive adventure!

Nestled at the heart of our vibrant community, our museum is a gateway to the wonders of the natural world, the marvels of technology, and the captivating mysteries of the universe. With hands-on exhibits, captivating displays, and engaging workshops, we invite visitors of all ages to explore, discover, and ignite their passion for science.

From the tiniest particles to the vast cosmos, join us on a journey that celebrates the beauty of knowledge and the power of inquisitive minds. Welcome to a world where science comes alive, and where every question sparks a new quest for understanding. Are you ready to jump in?

INFO

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Food and drink

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.
